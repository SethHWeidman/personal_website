Hi, I'm Seth

I'm a data scientist, educator, and technology writer. I love learning the latest tools, understanding everything from the math underlying them to their applications in business, and then teaching others about these tools.

I currently work for Metis, a Data Science Training company, teaching their immersive programs in Chicago and doing Data Science training for our corporate clients. As part of this role I speak frequently about Data Science (especially neural nets) at national conferences such as ODSC and local Meetups. I also invest in and have advised Chicago EdTech startups such as LearnMetrics and GetSet.

Prior to working for Metis, I have five years of experience in Data Science, Data Analytics, and Data Science Education at a variety of companies; read more here.
