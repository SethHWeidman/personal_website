Hi, I'm Seth

I teach, write about, and speak about Data Science, especially neural networks and Deep Learning. I've shared with the community how to build neural networks from scratch at conferences such as ODSC and PyData, taught libraries such as TensorFlow and Keras to my students at Metis, and have put together trainings for Metis' corporate clients on the leading applications of Deep Learning in industry.

Prior to this role I have five years of experience in Data Science, Data Analytics, and Data Science Education at a variety of companies; I also invest in and have advised Chicago EdTech startups such as Learnmetrics and GetSet. Read more here.
