About me

I am currently a data scientist at Sentilink, focused primarily on building machine learning models to stop synthetic fraud, and also growing the company and improving our data science practice.

In the seven years between college and beginning at Sentilink, I did variety of jobs in the data science and analytics fields, with a heavy emphasis on teaching and writing. I worked as everything from a technical analyst role in management consulting firm to a machine learning engineer at Facebook, helping to found the data science team at Trunk Club along the way. On the teaching and writing side, I taught at Metis (still the only accredited data science bootcamp) for 18 months, spoke at many community-oriented conferences (including 4 ODSCs), briefly worked as a developer advocate on the PyTorch team, and even wrote an introductory book on deep learning with O'Reilly.
